Platform Content Guidelines — Violence & Monetization

Section 4.2 — Violence and dangerous organizations

Rule (cited in demos)

"Content that glorifies or incites violence will be demonetized. Educational, documentary, scientific, or artistic content that discusses historical conflict without glorification is generally allowed for monetization when context is clear."

Enforcement ladder

  • Mild profanity / edge topics: age-restrict or limited ads
  • Clear glorification or incitement: demonetize or remove
  • Severe hate / real-world harm instructions: strike + removal

Burden of proof

Enforcement should cite a specific clause. Content that is merely edgy, controversial, or unpopular is not by itself a violation of this section.
